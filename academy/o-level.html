Program Overview

The NIPS O-Level Program follows the Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) curriculum. We offer support for both O-Level and IGCSE streams. Our approach focuses on developing the critical thinking and analytical skills required to secure top grades in Cambridge exams.

Understanding that O-Level students often struggle with the transition from local to international standards, our teachers provide bridged support to help them adapt. We focus heavily on past paper practice (ATP - Alternative to Practical) and structured answer writing.

Why Choose NIPS for O-Level?

  • CAIE Trained Faculty: Teachers who have attended official Cambridge training workshops and understand marking criteria.
  • Resource Material: Access to 10+ years of classified past papers, mark schemes, and examiner reports.
  • Mock Exams: Full-scale mock exams conducted under strict Cambridge-style conditions with detailed feedback.
  • Small Groups: Max 10 students per class for personalized attention and individual doubt clearing.
  • Practical Support: ATP (Alternative to Practical) focused training with specimen papers.

Who Should Join?

  • School Students: O-Level students from LGS, Beaconhouse, City School seeking extra support
  • Private Candidates: Self-study students preparing for Cambridge exams
  • Weak Subject Support: Students struggling with specific subjects like Physics or Add Maths
  • High Achievers: Students aiming for 8A* and world distinctions
