Program Overview

The NIPS O-Level Program follows the Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) curriculum. Whether you're attending an O-Level school in Pakistan or studying abroad in an international school, we provide the expert support you need to achieve A* grades.

For overseas Pakistani students in international schools following Cambridge curriculum, or those who want to supplement their school education, our online classes offer flexible timing and personalized attention from experienced CAIE teachers.

We focus heavily on past paper practice, ATP (Alternative to Practical) preparation, and developing the analytical skills that Cambridge examiners look for. Our teachers have attended official Cambridge training workshops.
