Program Overview

The NIPS Academy Matric Program provides comprehensive coaching for 9th and 10th grade students preparing for Federal Board (FBISE) and Punjab Board (BISE Lahore) examinations. Whether you're in Pakistan or abroad, our experienced teachers ensure personalized attention and excellent results.

We specialize in helping overseas Pakistani families keep their children connected to the Pakistani curriculum. Our online classes are scheduled at convenient times for students in the Gulf countries, United Kingdom, Europe, and North America.

With small batch sizes of maximum 15 students, we ensure every child receives individual attention. Our teachers have 10+ years of board exam experience and many are former paper checkers who know exactly what examiners look for.

Who Should Join?

  • 9th Class Students: Build a strong foundation before the crucial board year
  • 10th Class Students: Intensive preparation for SSC board exams
  • Overseas Pakistanis: Children of Pakistani families living abroad (UAE, KSA, UK, USA, Canada, Europe)
  • Private Candidates: Students appearing as private candidates for Federal Board exams
  • Weak Students: Special attention for students struggling with specific subjects
  • High Achievers: Push beyond 90% to secure positions in board exams
