Program Overview

The NIPS Academy Matric Program provides comprehensive coaching for 9th and 10th grade students preparing for Punjab Board (BISE Lahore) and Federal Board examinations. Our experienced teachers, most with 10+ years of board exam preparation experience, ensure that every student receives personalized attention and achieves their best possible results.

Unlike large tuition centers where students get lost in crowds of 50+, we maintain small batch sizes of maximum 15 students. This allows our teachers to identify and address each student's weak areas, provide regular feedback to parents, and ensure consistent improvement throughout the academic year.

We've helped hundreds of students from Johar Town, Model Town, and surrounding areas achieve A+ grades in their Matric exams. Many of our students have gone on to secure admissions in top colleges including Lahore College, Government College University, and more.

Who Should Join?

  • 9th Class Students: Build a strong foundation before the crucial board year
  • 10th Class Students: Intensive preparation for Matric board exams
  • Weak Students: Special attention for students struggling with specific subjects
  • High Achievers: Push beyond 90% to secure positions in board exams
  • Transfer Students: Catch up quickly if you've changed schools mid-year
