Program Overview

The NIPS Intermediate Program is designed for students who aim not just to pass, but to excel in their board exams and secure admission in top professional universities. We cover FSc (Pre-Medical & Pre-Engineering), ICS, and ICom streams with a dual focus on Board Exams and Entry Tests.

Intermediate is the career-defining stage. A few marks can change your future university path. That's why we employ only subject specialist teachers with Master's/MPhil degrees and proven track records. Our rigorous testing system ensures you are exam-ready months before the actual papers.

Why Join NIPS Intermediate?

  • Concept-Based Learning: We move beyond rote learning to deep conceptual understanding required for entry tests.
  • Entry Test Integration: Basic prep for MDCAT/ECAT concepts integrated into regular lectures from day one.
  • Individual Attention: Small batch sizes (max 12) allow teachers to focus on each student's progress.
  • Past Paper Sessions: Extensive practice of last 10 years' board papers with model answers.
  • Weekly Tests: Regular assessments to track progress and identify weak areas early.

Who Should Join?

  • Matric Graduates: Students who want a strong foundation for 11th and 12th
  • Medical Aspirants: Pre-Medical students preparing for MDCAT alongside board exams
  • Engineering Aspirants: Pre-Engineering students targeting ECAT and UET admission
  • Commerce Students: ICom students building foundation for CA/ACCA
