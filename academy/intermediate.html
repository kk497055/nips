Program Overview

The NIPS Intermediate Program prepares students for Federal Board (FBISE) and Punjab Board (BISE Lahore) HSSC examinations. We cover FSc Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, ICS, and ICom with a dual focus on board exams and entry test preparation (MDCAT/ECAT).

Intermediate is the career-defining stage. A few marks can determine your university path. Our subject specialist teachers (Master's/MPhil qualified) and small batch sizes ensure you get the focused attention needed to excel.

For overseas Pakistani families, we offer live online classes at convenient timings. Your child can continue Pakistani education while living in the Gulf, UK, USA, or anywhere in the world.
