Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and enrollment process.

You can enroll by filling out the enrollment form on this page, calling us at +92 321 5554125, or sending a WhatsApp message. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss course details, batch timing, and complete your registration.

We offer flexible batch timings to accommodate working professionals and students:

  • Morning Batch: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon Batch: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Evening Batch: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Weekend Batch: Saturday & Sunday

Classes are typically held 3 times per week, 2 hours per session.

Yes! Most of our courses are available in three modes:

  • Online (Live): Live interactive sessions via Zoom/Google Meet
  • In-Person: Physical classes at our Johar Town, Lahore campus
  • Hybrid: Combination of online and in-person sessions

Online classes follow the same curriculum and receive the same certificate as in-person classes.

We maintain a maximum batch size of 15 students for most courses (12 for Calligraphy). This ensures personalized attention, better interaction, and hands-on learning opportunities for every student.

Yes, all students who successfully complete a course receive a Certificate of Completion from NIPS Education Solutions. The certificate includes your name, course title, duration, and completion date. Digital and printed versions are available.

We offer flexible payment options:

  • Bank transfer (all major banks)
  • JazzCash / EasyPaisa
  • Cash payment at our office
  • Installment plans available for longer courses

Contact us for course fee details and available discounts.

Prerequisites vary by course:

  • Applied AI, Prompt Engineering, AI Graphics: Basic computer skills; no coding required
  • Cloud Computing: Basic programming knowledge recommended
  • IELTS, Spoken English: No prerequisites; all levels welcome
  • Calligraphy: No prerequisites; materials provided

Our campus is located in K Block, Johar Town, Lahore. It's easily accessible and has nearby parking facilities. For exact directions, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll share the Google Maps location.

We offer a flexible refund policy:

  • Before course starts: Full refund
  • Within first week: 80% refund
  • After first week: Credit towards future courses

You can also transfer your enrollment to a future batch if scheduling conflicts arise.
