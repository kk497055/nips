LLB Program Overview

The 5-Year LLB is the standard legal qualification in Pakistan for students who have completed their Intermediate (12 years of education). It is a comprehensive professional degree that covers Civil, Criminal, Corporate, and Constitutional Law.

Admission to any HEC-recognized law college REQUIRES passing the Law Admission Test (LAT) conducted by HEC. Our program guides you not only in selecting the right college but also prepares you to pass the LAT with high marks.

Why Choose Law?

  • Prestigious Career: Join the bar and practice as an Advocate in High Courts.
  • Judiciary Exams: Eligible to appear for Civil Judge / ASJ exams.
  • Corporate Sector: High demand for legal advisors in companies.
  • Civil Service: Strong foundation for CSS/PMS exams.

Admission Details

  • Degree:LLB (Hons)
  • Duration:5 Years
  • Eligibility:Intermediate (50%+)
  • Entry Test:HEC LAT (50 Marks)
  • Session:Annual / Semester
  • Colleges:PU / Private
