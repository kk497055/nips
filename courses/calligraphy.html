Course Overview

Our Calligraphy course is designed to teach you the timeless art of beautiful handwriting. Whether you're interested in traditional Islamic calligraphy, Urdu Nastaliq script, or modern English lettering, this course covers it all with hands-on practice and expert guidance.

Calligraphy is not just an art form - it's a meditation, a cultural heritage, and increasingly, a valuable skill for creating unique designs, wedding invitations, logos, and personalized gifts. This course will help you develop your own style while mastering classical techniques.

  • Nastaliq: The elegant Urdu and Persian calligraphy style

Week by Week

8-Week Learning Journey

Week 1-2

Foundations & Tools

  • Introduction to calligraphy history and styles
  • Understanding tools: pens, nibs, brushes, inks, papers
  • Basic strokes and warm-up exercises
  • Posture, grip, and workspace setup
  • Practice: Creating a stroke glossary
Week 3-4

Nastaliq & Urdu Script

  • Urdu alphabet in Nastaliq style
  • Letter connections and joining rules
  • Word formation and spacing
  • Writing poetry and prose
  • Project: Calligraphy of a famous Urdu verse
Week 5-6

Arabic Calligraphy

  • Naskh script fundamentals
  • Introduction to Thuluth style
  • Quranic verses and Islamic patterns
  • Geometric principles in Arabic calligraphy
  • Project: Decorative Arabic artwork
Week 7-8

Modern & Digital Calligraphy

  • Modern English calligraphy and brush lettering
  • Wedding invitation and card design
  • Scanning and digitizing calligraphy
  • Creating calligraphy for commercial projects
  • Final Project: Complete portfolio piece
