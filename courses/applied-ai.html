Course Overview

The Applied AI for Business course is designed to help professionals, entrepreneurs, and students leverage artificial intelligence to solve real-world business problems. You'll learn to use cutting-edge AI tools like ChatGPT, automate repetitive tasks, and make data-driven decisions.

This is not a theoretical course - every week you'll work on practical projects that you can immediately apply in your work or business. By the end of the program, you'll have a portfolio of AI-powered solutions and the confidence to implement AI in any business context.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Business Professionals: Managers, analysts, and executives looking to automate workflows
  • Entrepreneurs: Business owners wanting to leverage AI for growth
  • Freelancers: Professionals seeking to offer AI-enhanced services
  • Students: Anyone wanting to future-proof their career with AI skills
  • Marketing Professionals: Marketers looking to create AI-powered content

Complete Curriculum

12-Week Learning Journey

Module 1

Introduction to AI for Business

Week 1-2
  • Understanding AI, Machine Learning, and their business applications
  • Overview of AI tools landscape (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
  • Setting up your AI toolkit
  • Basic prompt engineering fundamentals
  • Project: Create your first AI-powered document
Module 2

Advanced Prompt Engineering

Week 3-4
  • Advanced prompting techniques (chain of thought, few-shot learning)
  • Creating custom instructions and personas
  • Prompt templates for different business scenarios
  • Quality control and refining AI outputs
  • Project: Build a library of business prompt templates
Module 3

Content Creation & Marketing

Week 5-6
  • AI-powered blog and article writing
  • Social media content generation and scheduling
  • Email marketing with AI assistance
  • SEO optimization using AI tools
  • Project: Create a complete content marketing campaign
Module 4

Business Automation

Week 7-8
  • Introduction to automation tools (Zapier, Make)
  • Automating email responses and customer service
  • Document processing and generation
  • Creating AI chatbots for business
  • Project: Automate a complete business workflow
Module 5

Data Analysis & Visualization

Week 9-10
  • Using AI to analyze spreadsheets and data
  • Creating reports and presentations with AI
  • Business intelligence with AI assistance
  • Market research and competitor analysis
  • Project: Analyze a real business dataset and present insights
Module 6

Capstone & Career Prep

Week 11-12
  • Integrating all skills into a comprehensive solution
  • AI ethics, limitations, and best practices
  • Building your AI portfolio
  • Career opportunities in AI
  • Capstone Project: Complete AI transformation project for a real business case
