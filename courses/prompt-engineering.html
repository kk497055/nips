Course Overview

The Prompt Engineering Masterclass teaches you how to effectively communicate with AI systems to achieve exceptional results. Whether you're using ChatGPT for content creation, Claude for analysis, or Midjourney for image generation, the quality of your output depends entirely on your prompting skills.

This course covers everything from basic prompting principles to advanced techniques like chain-of-thought prompting, few-shot learning, and system prompt design. You'll learn to craft prompts that consistently produce high-quality, accurate, and relevant outputs.

Why Prompt Engineering Matters

In the AI age, the ability to effectively communicate with AI tools is becoming as important as traditional computer skills. Those who master prompt engineering can accomplish tasks 10x faster than those who don't.

  • Business Professionals: Anyone using AI for work tasks
  • Developers: Those building AI-powered applications
  • Students: Anyone wanting to leverage AI for learning
  • Freelancers: Looking to offer AI-powered services

Complete Curriculum

6-Week Learning Journey

Week 1

Foundations of Prompt Engineering

  • Understanding how Large Language Models work
  • Anatomy of an effective prompt
  • Basic prompting patterns and structures
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them
  • Practice: Transform vague prompts into specific ones
Week 2

Advanced Text Prompting

  • Zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot prompting
  • Chain-of-thought and step-by-step reasoning
  • Role-playing and persona-based prompts
  • Structured output formats (JSON, tables, lists)
  • Practice: Create a content generation workflow
Week 3

Image Generation Prompting

  • Midjourney prompt structure and syntax
  • DALL-E prompting techniques
  • Style, lighting, and composition keywords
  • Negative prompts and refinement
  • Practice: Create a brand visual asset pack
Week 4

Business Applications

  • Email and communication templates
  • Marketing copy and social media content
  • Data analysis and report generation

Custom GPTs & Automation
